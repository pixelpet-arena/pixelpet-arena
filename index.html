Welcome to PixelPet Arena

Adopt a pixel pet, stake it to earn XP, and prepare it for epic battles in the arena. This NFT-powered experience is built on the Solana blockchain, designed for collectors, gamers, and Web3 adventurers alike.

Staking

Staking your NFT gives it purpose and growth. Lock your pet into the staking pool and earn XP rewards daily. XP is used to level up your pet, evolve it into stronger forms, and unlock exclusive content and bonuses.

  • Earn XP every day your pet is staked
  • XP can be used to evolve and enhance your pet
  • Unstake anytime and claim rewards

Token Utility

XP tokens are more than just numbers. They power the entire PixelPet universe. Here's what you can do with them:

  • 🎯 Evolve your pet into powerful and rare versions
  • 🗺️ Unlock new regions, challenges, and limited arenas
  • 🏆 Join tournaments and win exclusive NFTs or XP

FAQ

What is PixelPet Arena?

PixelPet Arena is a Solana-based NFT game where users can mint, stake, evolve, and battle with unique pixelated pets to earn rewards and unlock features.

How do I mint a pet?

Simply connect your wallet and click "Mint Your Pet". Your pet will appear in your wallet once the transaction confirms.

How does staking work?

When you stake a pet, it starts earning XP passively. The longer you stake, the more XP you earn. XP can be spent to evolve pets and access special content.

What wallet do I need?

You’ll need a Solana-compatible wallet like Phantom or Backpack. These are free and easy to set up.

When will battles be live?

Arena battles are in development and scheduled to go live in Q1 2026, bringing competitive gameplay and rewards.
