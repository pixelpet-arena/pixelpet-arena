Welcome to PixelPet Arena

Adopt a pixel pet, stake it to earn XP, and evolve it to compete in the arena. Powered by Solana.

Staking

Stake your PixelPet NFT and earn daily XP rewards. The longer you stake, the more powerful your pet becomes. XP can be used to evolve pets, unlock new arenas, and access rare customizations.

Token Utility

  • Use XP tokens to evolve your pet into stronger forms
  • Unlock new in-game features and battle arenas
  • Participate in tournaments & win prizes
  • Customize your pet’s appearance & powers
