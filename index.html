Welcome to PixelPet Arena

Adopt a pixel pet, stake it to earn XP, and prepare it for epic battles in the arena. This NFT-powered experience is built on the Solana blockchain, designed for collectors, gamers, and Web3 adventurers alike.

Staking

Staking your NFT gives it purpose and growth. Lock your pet into the staking pool and earn XP rewards daily. XP is used to level up your pet, evolve it into stronger forms, and unlock exclusive content and bonuses.

  • Earn XP every day your pet is staked
  • XP can be used to evolve and enhance your pet
  • Unstake anytime and claim rewards
