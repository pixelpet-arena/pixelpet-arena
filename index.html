Frequently Asked Questions

What is PixelPet Arena?
PixelPet Arena is a Web3-based NFT game where players mint, stake, and evolve pixel pets for battle and rewards.
How do I earn XP?
You earn XP by staking your NFT pets daily. XP is used to evolve pets and unlock exclusive game utilities.
What wallet should I use?
Use the Phantom wallet on Solana to connect and interact with PixelPet Arena securely.
Is it free to play?
You can explore the site for free. Minting and staking will require Solana in your wallet.
